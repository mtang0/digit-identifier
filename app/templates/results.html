Why Do We Miss Sometimes?

There are many reasons why our model may misclassify an image. Our model was trained on a specifically formatted dataset—see our advice for getting the best results—so it might not recognize some images. Also we hand labeled the images so any human error would contribute to the model messing up. Additionally, our model was trained with single digits and not with multi-digit numbers, which are much more common. Lastly this model was made in a limited amount of time.
